Problem statement: Blackboard Issues

Blackboard is difficult to navigate and students have trouble viewing and submitting assignments.

Affinity diagram: Blackboard Brainstorm

In our affinity diagram, we brainstormed around 20 different ideas, issues, connections, with our central problem. These problems were then assigned into groups in order to organize our ideas.

Team Personas: Combined Personas

Jordie Jordan is the fictional persona of a Professor in Psychology who is a novice with software and computers overall. This in turn, causes isues with working with students on assignments and notes.

Team Storyboards: Combined

Storyboard skits where our characters interact with our product.

Team Sketches: Blackboard alternative

Our team's sketches detail the functionality of assignment submission and grade depiction.

Team Paper Prototype: Blackboard alternative

Our team's paper prototype walks through the funcionality of the calendar, assignment submission, grade checking, and slide viewing features.

Team HiFi Prototype: Feature Walkthrough

Our team's Hifi prototype walks through the funcionality of the calendar, assignment submission, grade checking, and slide viewing features in a clickable online app.
